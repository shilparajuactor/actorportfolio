About Me

Shilpa Raju is a New York City based film and theatre performer. With roots in both India and the US, she infuses her work with a variety of cultural influences. By drawing on her professional background in STEM and Finance, she brings a unique and multifaceted perspective to her performances. Striving to bridge the gap between these diverse worlds, she uses her art as a tool for exploring identity and belonging.
